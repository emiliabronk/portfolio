About Me

Hello! I'm a Philosophy, Politics, and Economics major and Environmental Studies minor at the University of Pennsylvania.

I write about environmental and education policy, foreign affairs, particularly U.S.-China relations, and the EU. I also perform sketch and stand-up comedy with Mask & Wig and Simply Chaos. I'm currently working on my honors thesis exploring the culture of the Democratic Party and how it influences its use of celebrities in campaigns.

Looking ahead, I hope to work in the entertainment and political news space, ideally in research, writing, or representation.
